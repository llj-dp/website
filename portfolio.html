Capstone Project

Loro Piana: Heritage Studio - Curated Transparency & Co-Luxury

Project Context

Ultra-luxury consumers increasingly expect both discretion and supply chain transparency. The concept addresses a white space: high digital participation without sacrificing quiet luxury positioning.

  • Role: Individual project lead (research, strategy, concept, and presentation).
  • Audience: UHNWI clients under 45 and luxury decision makers.
  • Objective: Turn ownership into authorship through bespoke co-creation.

Key Insights

  • $1.5T global luxury market context
  • 75% of luxury buyers prioritize transparency
  • 80% of luxury purchases are digitally influenced
  • Primary research: 15 interviews + 30 surveys with UHNWI participants
+25% bespoke conversion target +15% UHNWI under-40 growth target +30% repeat purchase target

Process and Deliverables

1) Research and Diagnosis

Market sizing, SWOT, competitor mapping, and transparency trend analysis.

2) Strategy Definition

Positioning, target persona, and value proposition framing for co-authored quiet luxury.

3) Concept Design

Invitation-only bespoke platform, artisan collaboration, and blockchain-enabled traceability.

4) Go-to-Market

5Ps framework, campaign concept, and luxury-city rollout plan.

5) Performance Planning

Financial projection, KPI dashboard, and time-action calendar.
